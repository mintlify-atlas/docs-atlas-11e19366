---
title: "Creating a prescription"
description: "How doctors create prescriptions for residents"
---

Prescriptions are created by users with the **DOCTOR** or **ADMIN** role. The creation form creates a `Receta`, one `OrdenMedicamento`, and all `HoraProgramada` records in a single atomic database transaction.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the resident's detail page">
    Go to **Residentes**, find the resident, and click their name to open the detail page at `/residentes/<residente_id>/`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Nueva receta">
    Click the **Nueva receta** button. The form opens at `/recetas/nueva/<residente_id>/`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the prescription details">
    Complete the prescription header:

    | Field | Required | Notes |
    |---|---|---|
    | Fecha de inicio | Yes | Start date of the treatment |
    | Fecha de fin | No | Leave blank for open-ended prescriptions |
    | Observaciones | No | Clinical notes or special instructions |
    | Activa | Yes | Leave checked to activate the prescription immediately |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select or create a medication">
    Use the medication autocomplete field to search for an existing drug in the local catalog. The autocomplete queries three sources in order:

    1. **Local catalog** â€” drugs already registered in SIFA.
    2. **CIMA (AEMPS)** â€” the Spanish drug registry.
    3. **RxNorm (NIH)** â€” the US drug terminology database.

    If the medication is not found in any source, fill in the **Nuevo medicamento** section at the bottom of the form:

    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | Nombre | e.g. `Paracetamol` |
    | Potencia | e.g. `500 mg` |
    | Forma | e.g. `Tableta`, `Jarabe` |

    Submitting the form with these fields filled creates a new `Producto` in the catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set dose, route, and indications">
    Fill in the medication order fields:

    | Field | Example |
    |---|---|
    | Dosis | `1 tableta`, `5 ml` |
    | VĂ­a | `oral`, `IM`, `SC` |
    | Indicaciones | Free-text instructions for the caregiver |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add scheduled administration times">
    Click **Agregar hora** to add one or more scheduled times. For each time entry:

    - **Hora** â€” the administration time in HH:MM format (24-hour).
    - **DĂ­a de la semana** â€” optional. Leave blank to apply the schedule every day. Select a specific day (Lunâ€“Dom) to restrict the order to that day of the week only.

    You can add as many time entries as needed for the order.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Guardar receta**. If validation passes, SIFA:

    1. Creates the `Receta` and assigns it the next sequential number for this resident.
    2. Creates the `OrdenMedicamento` linked to the prescription.
    3. Creates all `HoraProgramada` records.

    You are redirected back to the resident's detail page with a success message showing the prescription number (e.g. `Receta #3 creada correctamente.`).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
At least one scheduled time is required. The form will not submit and will display the message "Agrega al menos una hora." if no times have been added.
</Note>

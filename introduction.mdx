# KIBEN – SIFA

SIFA (Sistema Integral de Farmacia y Administración) is a web-based healthcare management platform designed for nursing homes and elder care facilities. It centralizes resident records, medical prescriptions, medication administration tracking, inventory management, and caregiver assignments in one secure system.

## What SIFA does

SIFA solves the daily challenge of medication management in nursing homes by providing a structured, role-based workflow from prescription to administration.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Resident profiles" icon="person">
    Store complete medical profiles including allergies, prescriptions, and administration history for each resident
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prescription management" icon="file-medical">
    Doctors create prescriptions with medication orders, doses, and administration schedules
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily administration" icon="calendar-check">
    Staff track every medication dose — administered, omitted, or rejected — with a full audit trail
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inventory control" icon="boxes-stacked">
    Monitor medication stock levels per resident with automatic critical-stock alerts via Telegram
  </Card>
  <Card title="Caregiver assignments" icon="user-nurse">
    TENS staff assign caregivers to residents daily, controlling which residents each caregiver sees
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monthly reports" icon="chart-bar">
    Generate and export monthly administration reports as PDF for clinical records and audits
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who uses SIFA

SIFA is built around four user roles that mirror real nursing home workflows:

| Role | Spanish name | Primary responsibilities |
|------|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Admin** | Enfermera | Full system access, user management, stock updates |
| **Doctor** | Doctor | Create and manage prescriptions and medication orders |
| **TENS** | TENS | Manage caregiver assignments and restock medications |
| **Caregiver** | Cuidadora | View and mark daily medication administrations |

## Technology stack

SIFA is a Django 5.x web application backed by SQLite, with a companion Android app for mobile use.
